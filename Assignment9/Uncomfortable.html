Uncomfortable Art

For this project I decided to use Microsoft Paint to try my hand in digital painting. I had seen people create extreamly photo realistiic paintings in digital paint programs like this and that got me excited to try! I took inspiration from the famous painter Bob Ross and how he does his mountain vistas. It's amazing what you can acomplish with a limited color pallet mixed with some time and patience.
